About StrokePred

StrokePred is an innovative healthcare platform that uses artificial intelligence to assess stroke risk based on various health factors. Our mission is to make stroke prevention accessible to everyone through technology.

By analyzing factors such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, smoking habits, and other health indicators, our AI model provides personalized risk assessments that can help individuals make informed decisions about their health.
